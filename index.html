What ScamWarning detects

✓

Phishing & credential harvesting

Fake login pages, account verification scams, and credential theft attempts

✓

Financial fraud & investment scams

Guaranteed returns, crypto doubling schemes, fake trading platforms

✓

Tech support scams

Fake virus warnings, fraudulent support numbers, scare tactics

✓

Brand & government impersonation

Fake IRS, bank, and brand websites designed to steal your information

✓

Urgency manipulation & misleading ads

Countdown timers, fake scarcity, deceptive health claims, too-good-to-be-true offers

Privacy by design

We built ScamWarning to prove security tools don't need your data.

How it works

  • Local pattern matching runs first — no network call
  • API only contacted when threats are detected
  • Results cached locally for 1 hour
  • Anonymous install ID for rate limiting only

We never collect

  • Browsing history or page visits
  • Personal information or accounts
  • Passwords, cookies, or form data
  • IP addresses (not logged)

ScamWarning is a free, open-source project by MCPCIO.
